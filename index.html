⬛
2D Assetless Platformer
What I learned: Dash Mechanics & Time-Rewind Systems

A prototype built with no art assets, focused entirely on mechanics: the player dashes through specific walls to progress, and can rewind time to undo movement and re-attempt sections of the level. The challenge was making both systems work together cleanly, especially handling state when rewinding through a wall-dash.
